This Prefab is an interactable door. Perfect for 2d/3d enviroments with opening animations.
This the asset is a sci-fi themed door.
There is a custom added particle effect with several minor tweaks to give the door a psuedo breaking animation.
There is a 'blue wall' meant to offer as a potential loading and/or hiding of messy asset creation.

The script is a simple open and close animation with a slight delay on the closing to give more realistic visuals based on
collision detection.
